Jax Bot Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2025

1. Introduction

Jax (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is an independent, fan-made Discord bot for the mobile game mo.co by Supercell. This bot is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or approved by Supercell; it is operated under Supercell’s Fan Content Policy. Our services are hosted on bot-hosting.net. Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and how you can manage or delete your information.

2. Data We Collect

  • Discord Identifiers
    • User ID & Username: Collected when you interact with the bot to verify permissions and personalize responses.
    • Guild/Channel/Role IDs: Stored to configure announcement channels and role pings.
  • Interaction Data
    We process slash commands (e.g. /events, /next) in real time. These interactions are not stored in our database, aside from optional forwarding to a designated audit-log channel.
  • Configuration Settings
    Stored securely in our private SQLite database:
    • Announcement channel settings
    • Audit-log channel settings
    • Role assignments for events
    No other personal data, attachments, or message histories are stored.

3. Why We Need Your Data

  • Functionality: To announce in-game events accurately via slash commands.
  • Permissions: To verify who may use administrative commands.
  • Audit Logging: To provide an optional command history for server administrators.
  • Persistence: To remember your settings across bot restarts.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Event Announcements: Reading your settings to post alerts in the correct channel.
  • Command Processing: Executing slash commands in real time.
  • Logging: Forwarding interactions to an audit-log channel if enabled.

5. Data Sharing

  • Discord, Inc.: Via the Discord API for normal bot operations.
  • Audit-Log Channel: If enabled, your interactions (user ID, timestamp, command) are sent there.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your data with external parties, except as required by law.

6. Data Retention & Removal

  • Configuration Data: Retained until you remove it via slash commands:
    • /channel-delete
    • /delete-log
    • /delete-role by-id or /delete-role by-role
  • Audit Logs: Remain in your server’s channel until manually purged.
  • Deletion Requests: To delete all stored data, contact us (see Section 7). Requests will be fulfilled within 30 days.

7. Contact Us

We aim to respond within 72 hours.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Material changes will be reflected in the “Last updated” date, and may be announced via our support server. Please review this page periodically.
