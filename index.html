Jax Bot Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Last updated: April 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Jax (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is an independent, fan‑made Discord bot for the mobile game mo.co by Supercell. This bot is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or approved by Supercell; it is distributed under Supercell’s Fan Content Policy. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, with whom we share it, and how you can control or delete it.

2. Data We Collect

  • Discord Identifiers
    • User ID & Username: Collected when you interact with the bot to verify permissions and personalize responses.
    • Guild (Server) ID, Channel ID & Role ID: Stored to configure where announcements go, where logs are sent, and which roles to ping.
  • Command Content

    Any message you send that begins with our command prefix ($) is read by the bot. These messages are not stored in our database; they are logged in a designated Discord channel if you enable logging.

  • Configuration Settings
    • Announcement channel settings
    • Log channel settings
    • Role assignments for events

    No other personal data, attachments, or message histories are stored.

3. Why We Need Your Data

  • Operation & Functionality: To announce in‑game events accurately in your chosen channels and with the right role mentions.
  • Permissions Management: To verify who may use administrative commands (e.g. $start, $stop, $set-role).
  • Audit Logging: If you enable it, to provide an audit trail of commands for server administrators.
  • Service Configuration: To remember your settings between bot restarts.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Event Announcements: Reading your configuration to post alerts in the correct channel at the right time.
  • Command Handling: Processing your commands in real‑time to perform the requested actions.
  • Logging: Forwarding your commands to a designated log channel for transparency and troubleshooting (only if enabled).

5. Data Sharing

  • Discord, Inc.: Via the Discord API for normal bot operations.
  • Your Server’s Log Channel: If audit logging is enabled, your commands are shared to the configured channel.
  • No Third‑Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your data with external parties, except as required by law.

6. Data Retention & Removal

  • Configuration Data: Retained until you remove it via bot commands ($channel-delete, $delete-log, $delete-role) or request deletion.
  • Logs in Discord: Remain in your server’s log channel until manually purged.
  • Deletion Requests: To delete all data, contact us (see Section 7); we will process requests within 30 days.

  • Do not use the bot for illegal, defamatory, harassing, or malicious activities.
