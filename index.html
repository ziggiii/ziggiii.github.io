Jax Bot Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Jax (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is an independent, fan-made Discord bot for the mobile game mo.co by Supercell. This bot is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or approved by Supercell. Our services are hosted on bot-hosting.net. Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and how you can manage or delete your information.

2. Data We Collect

  • Discord Identifiers
    • User ID & Username: Collected on interaction to verify permissions and personalize responses.
    • Guild/Channel/Role IDs: Stored to configure announcement channels, log channels, and role pings.
  • Interaction Data
    We process slash commands (e.g. /next, /loadout) in real time. We do not store the content of those commands outside of optional audit-logging.
  • Configuration Settings
    Stored securely in our private SQLite databases:
    • Announcement channel settings
    • Audit-log channel settings
    • Role assignments for events
    • News channel settings
  • Custom Loadouts
    When you create a loadout via /create-loadout, we store:
    • Category (Dojo/Rift/World)
    • Name of your loadout
    • Weapon, gadgets, passives, rings
    • Optional comment
    • Timestamp and your user ID
  • Like/Dislike Votes
    When you vote on community loadouts, we store:
    • Loadout ID
    • Your user ID
    • Your vote (+1, –1)
    • Timestamp
  • Telemetry & Analytics
    We optionally track command usage for statistics:
    • Command name
    • Guild ID, User ID
    • Timestamp
    This data is stored in a separate telemetry database and used only for aggregate usage metrics (unique users, server count, command counts).

3. Why We Need Your Data

  • Functionality: To deliver event announcements, loadout management, and news features correctly.
  • Permissions: To verify administrative access for certain commands.
  • Persistence: To remember your settings and loadouts across restarts.
  • Community Voting: To prevent multiple votes and generate leaderboards.
  • Analytics: To improve bot reliability and feature usage insights.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Event & News Announcements: Posting alerts in the correct channels.
  • Command Processing: Executing slash commands in real time.
  • Audit Logging: Forwarding to designated log channels if enabled.
  • Loadout Management: Saving, displaying, and deleting your custom loadouts.
  • Voting System: Recording your votes and updating like/dislike counts.
  • Telemetry: Aggregating command usage for dashboards and bot health monitoring.

5. Data Sharing

  • Discord, Inc.: Via the Discord API for bot operations.
  • Audit-Log Channel: If enabled, your interactions (user ID, timestamp, command) are sent there.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your data with external parties, except as required by law.

6. Data Retention & Removal

  • Configuration & Loadout Data: Retained until you remove it via slash commands:
    • /channel-delete, /delete-log, /delete-role
    • /delete-loadout or the delete button under /show-loadout
  • Voting Records: Retained until you delete your loadout (votes cascade) or on request.
  • Telemetry Data: Aggregated summary data retained indefinitely; raw logs purged after 90 days.
  • Deletion Requests: To delete all stored data, contact us (see Section 7). Requests will be fulfilled within 30 days.
